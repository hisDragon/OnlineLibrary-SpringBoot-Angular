ABOUT US

Our goal is to provide a page on the web for every book ever published. The ultimate goal of the Open Library is to make all the published works of humankind available to everyone in the world. Achieving it will require the participation of librarians, authors, government officials and technologists.

Open Library is one of the project made for the people so that they can use the knowledge from anywhere through e-books.This project was made on September 2020 by a team of 5 members with a vision "Feed the mind".
